Why Kenome?
As part of regular business, enterprises generate a lot of dark data -- unstructured data in the form of employee emails, customer communication, presentations, manuals, maintenance and research notes, etc. Data relevant to the enterprise is also present on the Web in the form of blogs, social media discussions, news, etc. The heterogeneous, unstructured, and scattered nature of this dark data poses significant challenges in harnessing them for improved decision making.

Kenome brings years of world-class research and industrial experience to help unlock the true value of unstructured dark data.
